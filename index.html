    What's in the air?

    From acid rain, to accelerated aging of the lungs, respiratory difficulty, and stillbirth; air pollution is a significant threat to life and living.

    With the rise of climate-induced diseases / infections and debilitating silence of the government on issues of air pollution, citizens need to be educated. Lagosians need to know the true state of their atmosphere and the extent of pollution in the air they breath. Civil society organizations need empirical evidence to challenge policy makers and journalists need data to tell compelling stories on air pollution.

    About

    Code for Nigeria is a non-profit civic technology movement. A network of civic-minded volunteers who contribute their talents, skills, expertise, and community knowledge as technological tools to improve public service, bring governments to account, and promote informed (data-driven) decision-making; leveraging open data + media, and backed by a core (full-time) tech team.
